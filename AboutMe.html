I am a junior at Cornell University, where I'm studying electrical and computer engineering with a minor in computer science. My academic areas of interest include Computer Software, Computer Architecture, and Computer Vision. Being part of a research group on campus, I am exploring different ways to make FPGA reconfigurable computing accessible for mainstream programmers. In addition to my research, I am the publicity director of IEEE which allows me to promote ECE events throughout the Cornell community. I am also a member of Cornell Ambassadors, through which I get to be a liaison between current students and prospective students. I enjoy visiting the beautiful trails that run through the campus’s gorges, trying out new food in Ithaca Commons. During the winter, you can find me cheering for the Cornell hockey team. Go Big Red!

I was born in Kolkata, India and moved to the U.S. when I was 7 years old. I grew up in a town called Sharon, Massachusetts which is about 30 minutes away from Boston. As a Bostonian, my love for the Patriots and Red Sox can never be questioned along with my penchant for Dunkin Donuts. As an Indian-American, I am able to be a part of two very different cultures and integrate them both into my everyday life.

Dance is my first passion. I started out by learning Indian classical dance before I moved to ballet and modern. After finishing my dance training in high school, I continue to practice, choreograph, and perform dance in my free time. I am an avid reader and enjoy reading psychological thrillers and historical dramas. My list of favorite books includes: The Kite Runner, Gone Girl, A Thousand Splendid Suns, and The Girl On the Train. I also enjoy playing the flute, swimming, and watching sci-fi/action movies.

Volunteering is an important aspect of my life because it has taught me how to give back to my community. For four years, I have worked as a cardiology unit volunteer in my local hospital. I have participated in numerous renovation projects around my hometown through my high school community service group. Additionally, I volunteer in Mother Teresa’s Charity Home, Missionaries of Charity, whenever I visit Kolkata, India. Volunteering has allowed me to work hard for the welfare of my community, and I have gained so many fond memories along the way.
